Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-23 — Draft. Legal review pending before public launch. The clauses below are implementation-complete pending counsel sign-off.

What we collect

  • Your Google account identifier (sub), email address, and YouTube channel ID/title
  • YouTube Analytics data: per-video metrics, retention curves, audience activity windows, demographics (aggregated)
  • Audience demographics: YouTube audience-demographics data (age ranges, country distributions, device types) is aggregated and anonymised by YouTube before delivery via the YouTube Analytics API. CreatorClip stores only the aggregated figures returned by the API — no individual viewer personally identifiable information (PII) is received, stored, or processed.
  • Video source files you upload (deleted within 72 hours of processing)
  • Your interactions with clip candidates (upvote/downvote/skip/trim) to improve personalisation

How we use it

All data is used exclusively to provide the AutoClip service — generating personalised clip candidates and content recommendations for your channel. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train models for other users.

YouTube API data

AutoClip accesses YouTube data via the YouTube API Services under your authorisation. Your YouTube data is handled per the Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke access at any time via Google Security Settings. Analytics data is refreshed periodically and not retained indefinitely.

Limited Use disclosure

AutoClip's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, AutoClip:

  • limits its use of YouTube API data to providing and improving the user-facing features described in this policy;
  • does not transfer YouTube API data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve those features, for security purposes, or to comply with applicable law;
  • does not use YouTube API data for serving advertisements; and
  • does not allow humans to read your YouTube API data unless you have given consent, it is necessary for security or to comply with applicable law, or the data has been aggregated and anonymised.

International transfers

All sub-processors listed above are US-based. Personal data transferred from the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom to these processors is governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in accordance with GDPR Art. 46(2)(c), as recorded in docs/SUBPROCESSORS.md.

None of the sub-processors listed above are currently self-certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework (DPF) for this account relationship. SCCs are therefore the operative transfer mechanism for all vendors. This will be updated if any vendor becomes DPF-certified and that certification is confirmed at dataprivacyframework.gov.

Data retention

  • Source video files: deleted within 72 hours of processing
  • Rendered clip files: retained until you delete them or your account
  • Analytics data: refreshed periodically; deleted on account deletion or if authorisation cannot be re-verified within 30 days (per YouTube API Services Terms §III.E.4.b)
  • OAuth tokens: stored encrypted; deleted on account deletion or token revocation

Your rights

You may request deletion of all your data at any time via the account deletion option in your profile settings. This revokes our OAuth access, deletes all stored data (including telemetry logs), and purges all media files. The deletion record we retain contains no personal data — only an internal identifier as evidence the erasure occurred.

Access & portability: you may request a copy of your data at any time. AutoClip generates a machine-readable JSON export of your profile, channel DNA, videos and metrics, clips, feedback, outcomes, chat history, and billing records, with download links for your rendered clips. The export is prepared on request and made available as a download from your account.

EU/UK residents: you have additional rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR, including the right to access, rectification, restriction of processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at reesepludwick@gmail.com.

California residents (CCPA)

This section applies to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

Categories of personal information collected: identifiers (Google account ID, email, YouTube channel ID); internet or other electronic network activity (clip interactions, session data); commercial information (billing records); inferences drawn from this information (channel DNA, content style profile).

Purposes of collection: to provide and improve the AutoClip service as described in this policy.

We do not sell or share your personal information. AutoClip does not sell personal information to third parties, and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising purposes.

Your CCPA rights: you have the right to know about personal information collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to correct inaccurate personal information, and the right to opt out of sale or sharing (not applicable — we do not sell or share). To exercise these rights, contact reesepludwick@gmail.com.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

AutoClip is a general-audience service not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction — 16 in EU member states that have adopted the GDPR Article 8 digital-consent age of 16). We screen for minimum age at account creation: users must affirmatively confirm they are 13 or older before the sign-in flow can be completed.

If you believe that a child under the applicable minimum age has created an account, please contact us at reesepludwick@gmail.com. We will promptly investigate and, if confirmed, delete the account and all associated personal data.

This section constitutes AutoClip's COPPA notice for purposes of the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (16 CFR Part 312, as amended effective 2025-06-23). CreatorClip does not have actual knowledge that it has collected personal information from users under 13.

Cookies

AutoClip uses strictly necessary cookies only:

  • Session JWT cookie — authenticates your session; HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax, Secure in production.
  • OAuth state cookie — prevents CSRF during the Google OAuth flow; short-lived, deleted after authentication completes; HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax.

No analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or third-party cookies are set. Because only strictly-necessary cookies are used, no consent banner is required under the ePrivacy Directive.

Security

OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard symmetric encryption. All data is transmitted over TLS. We minimise stored PII to what is strictly necessary for the service.

Breach contact

In the event of a personal data breach, you may be notified at the email address registered with your account. Data breach queries and reports can be directed to reesepludwick@gmail.com. Once a Data Protection Officer (DPO) is designated, this section will be updated with the DPO contact and the relevant supervisory authority referral.
